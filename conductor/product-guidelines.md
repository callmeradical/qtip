# qtip Product Guidelines

## Design Principles
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, high-signal, developer-centric.
- **Interactivity:** Deterministic, consistent, and provides clear, immediate feedback.
- **Tone:** Professional, direct, technical, yet helpful.

## Prose & Documentation
- Use active voice and concise technical language.
- Ensure all technical documentation includes clear examples and use cases.
- Maintain a focus on "why" things are built the way they are, not just "how".

## UX & Developer Experience
- Prioritize ease of integration for autonomous agents.
- Maintain human-readability of all machine-readable output.
- All errors should be descriptive and actionable.

## Branding
- **Name:** Always lowercase (qtip).
- **Core Value:** "Objective Truth in Software Evaluation."
